Frequently asked questions
What do you offer?
We offer unique immersive technology to elevate your events. Our services range from harnessing the immersive realm of Augmented Reality to incorporating a striking 30ft. audio-reactive LED curtain. Explore the possibilities with us.
What is a typical timeline?
Our timelines depends on the scale and the service you are looking for. This can range between few days and a few months to accommodate specific customizations. However, keep your eye out for our ready-to-use immersive templates too.
What sets you apart?
Oh, where do we start? Our experiences are curated with YOU at the centre. We offer both turn-key solutions, as well as highly personalised experiences. Our prices are highly competitive for the quality of technology we use and our attention to detail.
What sectors do you specialize in?
We specialize in curating pop-ups, music releases, club nights, and networking events. We are also available for more intimate events based on what you are looking for. As long as you have a group of people to entertain, we are here to help.
Can you accommodate specific customization requirements?
Absolutely! We want to make “templatized” experiences way where clients can create their own branding through AR, a 3D camera experience, and so on. However, there are additional charges depending on the customization.
